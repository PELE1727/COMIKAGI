          ABOUT COMIKAGI

          Minerals mined in COMIKAGI

          Key mined and still mining minerals in COMIKAGI are Cassiterite and coltan.

          History of mining in COMIKAGI

          Cooperative COMIKAGI is a mining cooperative with its headquarters in the Gakenke district, Ruli sector, Gikingo cell. It has been involved in mining for almost 35 years since 1989. Kababara perimeter is one of the six (6) mining licenses that COMIKAGI owns and is one where mining started. The president of Cooperative COMIKAGI is Kayitesi Beatrice (0783419757/comikagi2030@gmail.com). Mining activities have been conducted through underground operations.

          Production status

          COMIKAGI production is good ranging between 10,000kg to 20,000kg. The cassiterite is more dominant than coltan in pegmatite ore.

          Adding value to mined minerals

          Currently COMIKAGI uses Magnetic separator machines which use strong permanent magnets or electromagnets to remove ferrous contaminants from concentrate ores from the field based on magnetic properties. They are crucial for industries like mining because utilizing magnetic drums, rolls, or belts to separate materials, with capacities ranging from fine particles to large debris.

          Future projection

          Future project of COMIKAGI is Smelting process which will be used to extract pure metals from their mineral ores. Unlike simple melting, which only changes a substance's state from solid to liquid, smelting uses intense heat and chemical reducing agents to break the bonds between the metal and other elements like oxygen or sulfur.

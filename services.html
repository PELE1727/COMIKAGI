SERVICES

Mining

Mining is the extraction of geological materials and minerals from the surface of the Earth. The Cassiterite and coltan are key minerals found in Pegmatite ore hosting in metasedimentary rocks.

Laboratory Work

Mining laboratory work involves the scientific testing and analysis of geological samples (ore, rock, tailings) to determine their chemical composition, grade, and physical properties. COMIKAGI uses methods like XRF in assaying to guide exploration and ensure quality control.

Mineral Processing

Mineral processing, known as ore dressing, is the initial stage of extractive metallurgy that uses physical and chemical methods to separate valuable minerals from raw ore and waste rock. It increases the concentration and grade of the desired material.

Market

The general minerals market size has grown strongly in recent years. Metal and mineral prices are determined by supply and demand, influenced by global economic conditions, production costs, and commodity trading dynamics.

Hospitality

Hospitality is the friendly, generous reception and entertainment of guests. In COMIKAGI, it encompasses both personal acts of kindness and professional provision of food, drink, and accommodation.
